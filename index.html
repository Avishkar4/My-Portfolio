About

Curious about why businesses work.

I'm Avishkar Patil, a finance student with a singular focus: becoming an equity research analyst. My obsession isn't just with numbers — it's with the story behind the numbers. Why did margins compress? What drove that order growth? Is the market pricing in the right narrative?

That curiosity started in college and hasn't stopped. I've gone deep on companies like Swiggy, built sector analyses, and co-founded a business — all because I want to understand how capital, decisions, and markets interact at ground level before I analyze them from above.

Currently clearing NISM certifications and sharpening my valuation toolkit. Goal: Equity Research Analyst. Building toward it one company at a time.
